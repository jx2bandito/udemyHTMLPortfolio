Jacob's Best Movies List

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Terminator 2: Judgment Day

Classic action movie with many iconic scenes.

Kimi No Na Wa

Beautiful anime film that will, to quote my friend, "molly whop you in the feels."

Monty Python and the Holy Grail

Absurd, low-budget, non-sensical, genius, and hilarious. Monty Python's take on the Arthurian legend.
